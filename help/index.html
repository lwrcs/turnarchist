Turnarchist

Instructions

Turnarchist is turn-based, like chess!

Monsters only move when you do, so take your time deciding what to do next.

Turnarchist is a roguelike, which means:
All progress is lost upon death.

Controls:

Movement and attacking: Arrow Keys or WASD

Mouse movement: Left click in a direction to step; hold click to keep moving

Inventory: I or clicking the Inventory button (bottom-right)

Use / equip selected item: Space or left click an item in the quickbar/inventory

Drop item: Q or drag an item outside the inventory UI

Map: M or click the minimap to open the full map

Context menu (actions/examine): Right click

Close menus: Esc

Chat: C (or /)

Zoom in/out: + / -
