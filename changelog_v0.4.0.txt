6/1/2026 – v0.4.0 is out!

A big one — catching up from v0.3.1. New environments, new enemies, a spell + mana system, skills, smithing, armor, a bestiary, a sewer sidepath, diving, vending, right-click menus, color-coded keys, save/load v2, desktop builds, and a long list of fixes.

## New Major Features

🪄 **Spell system** – cast spells via spellbooks and spell scrolls. New SpellBeam animations and a dedicated targeting UI. Scrolls drop from tombstones and wizards.
🔮 **Mana system** – reworked mana with a dedicated mana bar. Mana potions added (stackable).
📈 **Skills + skills menu** – full skill system with a menu, balancing tweaks, and weapon skill requirements.
📖 **Bestiary** – browse encountered enemies with healthbars, hit warnings, beam previews, themes, vertical view, touch scrolling, and fading. Right-click any enemy → "View in Bestiary."
🔨 **Smithing interface** – dedicated smithing menu with improved appearance.
🛡️ **Armor system** – chestplate, shoulder plates, gauntlets, backplate added.
🏛️ **Pre-designed Castle** – hand-built castle structure. Final castle room holds the mainpath key. Castle bosses are now guarded.
🛒 **Vending machine** – purchase items in-world (with cost adjustments).
💎 **XP crystals** – pick up XP as crystals.
🖱️ **Right-click context menus** – desktop + mobile. Attack enemies, examine items, interact with pushables, default actions in inventory, plus submenus.
🚇 **Sewer sidepath** – brand new sidepath environment with its own structure and tunnels.
🌲 **Dark Forest** – dark variant biome with dark fir trees, dark grass, dark plants, and matching particles.
💾 **Save/Load v2** – fully reworked saving. Autosaves periodically, handles iOS background, restores curses/bleed/poison/shielded state/castle boss rooms, includes a roundtrip validator. Save/load is back on!
